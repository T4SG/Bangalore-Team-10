Welcome to Pratham

Pratham is one of the largest non-governmental organisations in India.It works towards the provision of quality education to the underprivileged children in India. Established in Mumbai in 1994 to provide pre-school education to children in slums, it now has activities in 21 states of India and has supporting chapters in the United States, UK, Germany and UAE.

Problem

Pratham believes that the problem is grave, but solutions exist.
Even after 68 years of independence, India is beset by serious problems with its education system. Numerous research studies and surveys have substantiated these problems and prove that immediate attention and action is required.
